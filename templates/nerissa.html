Biography

Nerissa Ravencroft is a member of Hololive English -Advent-, known by fans as the Devilish Diva of Hololive. Her character combines gothic elegance, emotional vulnerability, and romantic charisma.

With her deep, powerful singing voice, she weaves stories of sorrow, love, and hope, creating performances that deeply move her audience. Despite her mature and teasing personality, Nerissa has a soft, sincere side that makes her highly relatable and beloved among her fans, the Jailbirds.

Drawing from a lore of imprisonment and longing, Nerissa embraces themes of freedom through music, positioning herself as one of Hololive EN's standout vocalists and emotional storytellers.
